Privacy Framework

Privacy Policy

XySide Matrix is designed around privacy-first product architecture and transparent disclosure. This policy explains how data is handled across the XySide network.

Universal Scope

This privacy policy applies universally to https://xyside.com and all present or future subdomains (including calc.xyside.com, tools.xyside.com, ai.xyside.com, and any upcoming web apps launched within our network ecosystem).

The Zero-Server / Local Processing Guarantee

Our absolute architectural bottom line is client-side sandbox execution. Any variables, financial data, input text, text frameworks, configurations, images, or media assets you upload or enter into any tool across our matrix are processed 100% locally within your browser. We run a completely serverless front-end pipeline; we do not operate databases to harvest, inspect, or store your data.

Information We May Receive

Because XySide tools are designed to execute in-browser, we generally do not receive or retain the contents you enter into supported tools. We may receive limited operational signals that help us understand aggregate traffic quality, service health, and ad delivery performance.

Cookies & Tracking

We partner with Google AdSense for contextual and personalized advertising and Google Analytics for aggregated, anonymized user traffic telemetry. These services may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to measure audience behavior, improve advertising relevance, prevent fraud, and maintain reporting accuracy.

Third-Party Services

Some pages within the XySide network may contain content, scripts, or links operated by third parties. Those providers maintain their own privacy practices, and your use of their services is governed by their respective terms and policies.

Data Security Principles

We prioritize browser-native execution, modern HTTPS deployment, minimal data handling, and least-privilege architecture. No internet-facing system can guarantee absolute security, but our baseline design goal is to reduce collection by design rather than rely on downstream storage controls.

Children's Privacy

XySide services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through a third-party service linked from our properties, contact the relevant service provider directly.

International Access

XySide properties may be accessed from multiple jurisdictions. By using the network, you acknowledge that ad, analytics, and infrastructure partners may process limited technical metadata in accordance with their applicable global data frameworks.

Your Choices

  • You can limit cookies or tracking through your browser settings and consent controls.
  • You can avoid entering sensitive information into tools unless you are comfortable doing so locally.
  • You can discontinue use of any XySide property at any time.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect new tools, regulatory requirements, advertising partners, or architectural changes across the XySide ecosystem. Continued use of the network after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
